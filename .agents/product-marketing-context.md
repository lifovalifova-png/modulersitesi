## What ModülerPazar Does

ModülerPazar connects buyers (contractors, individuals, real estate developers) with verified modular construction firms across Turkey. Categories covered:
- Prefabrik evler (prefabricated homes)
- Konteyner ev (container houses)
- Tiny house
- Çelik yapı (steel structures)
- Ahşap modüler (timber modular buildings)
- Modüler ofis & sosyal tesis (modular offices & facilities)

The platform is NOT a transaction marketplace — it facilitates discovery, quote requests (teklif talebi), and direct contact between parties. ModülerPazar earns from seller subscriptions and featured listings, not transaction commissions.

## Target Audiences (ICPs)

### ICP 1: Modüler Üretici Firma (Seller Side — Primary Revenue)
- **Who:** Turkish modular construction manufacturing firms, typically 10-100 employees
- **Profile:** Family-owned or SME, 5-25 years in business, mostly Anatolian industrial cities (Sakarya, Kocaeli, Bursa, Ankara, Konya, Kayseri)
- **Decision Maker:** Firma sahibi (owner) or satış müdürü (sales director); rarely a CMO — marketing decisions happen informally
- **Current Marketing Channels:** Mostly word-of-mouth, 0-1 sales reps, basic website (often outdated), some Instagram presence, occasional fair attendance (Yapı Fuarı, MOBİSAD)
- **Pain Points:**
  - Unpredictable lead flow — boom and bust cycles
  - Hard to reach contractors outside their region
  - Spend on Google Ads with poor targeting
  - No structured way to showcase past projects
  - Difficulty competing with larger firms on visibility
- **What ModülerPazar Solves:** Continuous warm lead pipeline, geographic expansion without sales team, project portfolio showcase, verified-firm credibility badge
- **Free Tier:** 3 listings, 30-day duration, 3 renewals — designed to prove value before upgrade
- **Conversion Trigger:** Receiving 1-2 qualified quote requests through the platform usually proves ROI

### ICP 2: Müteahhit / Yatırımcı (Buyer Side — Demand Generator)
- **Who:** Turkish general contractors, individual investors building villas/cottages/sites, real estate developers, public sector procurement officers
- **Profile:** Project-based purchasing, often urgent timelines, comparing 3-5 firms before deciding
- **Search Behavior:** "prefabrik ev fiyatları", "konteyner ev İstanbul", "modüler villa yapan firmalar" — long-tail, location-modified, comparison-intent queries
- **Pain Points:**
  - Difficult to compare firms apples-to-apples
  - Fear of low-quality firms / scams (high-ticket purchase)
  - Want to see real prices before contacting (modüler sektör fiyat opaklığı yüksek)
  - Need delivery timeline visibility
- **What ModülerPazar Solves:** Centralized comparison, verified firm badges, customer reviews (verified buyers only), quick quote requests to multiple firms
- **Will Always Be Free:** This side never pays — they are the demand that justifies seller subscriptions

### ICP 3: Şahıs Satıcı (Individual / 2nd-Hand Seller)
- **Who:** Individuals selling used containers, second-hand prefab units, or urgently liquidating modular assets
- **Smaller segment but useful for "acil satılık" (urgent sale) badge content and SEO long-tail
- **Free Tier:** 1 listing, 30-day duration, 1 renewal — kept intentionally limited

## Brand Voice

- **Trust-anchored:** "Onaylı firma" (verified firm) is a core trust signal. Every claim should reinforce this.
- **Sector-fluent:** Use proper Turkish construction terms (müteahhit, taahhüt, anahtar teslim, ruhsat). Don't translate from English SaaS conventions.
- **Practical not aspirational:** Buyers need answers (price ranges, delivery times, regulations), not lifestyle imagery.
- **Direct:** Turkish modular sector buyers are direct, value time, dislike fluff. Match this register.
- **Avoid:** "Disrupt", "platform", "ecosystem", "synergy" — Turkish equivalents that sound MBA-translated. Use simpler "pazaryeri", "üretici firma", "alıcı".

## Constraints & Sensitivities (CRITICAL)

### Legal / Regulatory (Turkey-specific)
- **KVKK Compliance:** All marketing data collection must reference KVKK (Turkish GDPR equivalent). Email outreach must include unsubscribe + identity disclosure.
- **Mesafeli Satış Sözleşmesi:** Even though ModülerPazar is not a transaction marketplace, any future paid plan signup needs proper distance sales contract (in scope for Phase 3).
- **İmar Mevzuatı (Zoning Law):** Modular building legality varies by municipality. All content about "yasal mı" (is it legal?) questions must reference the local zoning authority, not give blanket answers.

### Sector-Specific Sensitivities
- **Price-specific content ages fast** — Steel and timber prices fluctuate monthly. Avoid putting prices in evergreen content; instead reference "fiyat aralığı 2024 itibarıyla" with disclaimers.
- **Earthquake sensitivity** — Post-2023 earthquake content must be respectful, factual, never exploiting tragedy for marketing angles.
- **Family-owned firm dynamics** — Marketing copy targeting firm owners should respect generational legacy, not position ModülerPazar as "disrupting" their established business.

## What This Skill Set Should NEVER Do for ModülerPazar
